Too many times have I stepped in my dogs poop, or been too lazy to keep their space clean. Well if I'm having that problem, surely others are too.. combined with my drive for entrepreneurship, I created AR Doody Duty L.L.C.

Now I can't exactly say this is a business I ever imagined myself in, but I have always loved animals, so being able to ensure their happiness and healthiness for others is certainly something I find satisfying.

I'm devoted to growing this service, and that means making sure that my clients are consistently satisfied with the level of cleanliness I am able to provide for their yards or event spaces.

My two girls, Daisy and Tayla, are poop machines! The amount of doody these two dogs generate is crazy! Their pen was always full of it, and it made walking around the backyard hazardous. I have them to thank for my inspiration to create this service. When looking for a business to start, I immediately honed in on a pet-based business, as I love animals of all shapes and sizes. Problem being: I have little money and work a full-time job.

So in an effort to find I low-cost business that I can work during my two off-days a week, I stumbled upon this Pet Waste Management idea. My dream is that I quit my full-time job, and expand my business to do more to care for pets than just pick up their waste. With the help of my clients, I hope to expand my services to Pet Day-Care/Boarding, Dog-Walking, Dog-Training, and Animal Rescue.
